Product Description

Our premium oak hardwood flooring is crafted from sustainably sourced white oak, offering exceptional durability and a beautiful natural grain pattern. Each plank is precision-milled for a perfect fit and finished with a ultra-durable matte coating that resists scratches and wear while maintaining the wood's natural beauty.

  • 100% solid white oak construction
  • 3/4" thickness for superior stability
  • Pre-finished with 7-layer UV-cured aluminum oxide coating
  • Available in widths from 3" to 7"
  • Low-VOC, environmentally friendly finish
  • 25-year residential warranty

Technical Specifications

Material Solid White Oak
Thickness 3/4" (19mm)
Width 3", 5", 7" options
Length 12" - 84" random lengths
Finish Matte UV-cured aluminum oxide
Janka Hardness 1360 (excellent durability)
Moisture Content 6-8% at time of shipping
Warranty 25-year residential, 10-year commercial
