Information We Collect

At BuildCraft, we collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you. This includes both personal and non-personal data that helps us understand our customers better while respecting your privacy.

Personal Data

Name, email, address, phone number, and payment information when you make a purchase.

Usage Data

Information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

Cookies

Small data files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience.

Use of Your Information

The information we collect serves multiple purposes to enhance your experience with BuildCraft. We're committed to using your data responsibly and transparently.

Order Processing

To process and fulfill your purchases and requests

Customer Support

To respond to your inquiries and provide service

Improvements

To enhance and personalize your experience

Communications

To send important notices and updates

Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in limited circumstances with trusted partners and service providers. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Service Providers

Legal Requirements
